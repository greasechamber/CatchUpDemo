CatchUp Find out what you missed!

 CatchUp is designed to help you stay in touch with your loved ones who may have different schedules than you. It aggregates popular posts from multiple different sites in one place, to save you time and allow you to quickly find out what you missed while you were away

Simply add an account from any of our supported sites to your CatchUp List, and CatchUp will automatically curate a feed for you, comprised of the most popular or important posts from your desired timeframe.

CatchUp also has a built in messaging system, to allow you to easily get in touch with any user who's posts you've added. You can quickly view other's schedules at a glance, making it easier than ever to make plans with them.

Our goal is to keep you connected to the people around you by keeping you in the loop.
